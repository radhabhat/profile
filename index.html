Brief Biography


A hearty Welcome! 

I am an Assistant Professor at Dept. of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), India.

I have spent five colorful years of my student life at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur, Karnataka, India doing PhD (with Prof. N R Sunitha) and Master of Tecnology (with Prof. N R Sunitha).

Born in a remote village of Karnataka, I have graduated from Govt. S.K.S.J.T.I, Bengaluru and completed my initial studies from Kumarpur Hateswar High School and Gobardhanpur Pramathanath Vidyayatan, West Bengal. Apart from science, I have artistic inclinations towards photography and writing.
