Brief Biography


A hearty Welcome! 

I am an Assistant Professor at Dept. of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), India.

I have spent five colorful years of my student life at Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur, Karnataka, India doing Ph.D (with Prof. N R Sunitha) and Master of Technology (M.Tech) (with Prof. N R Sunitha).

Born in a remote village of Karnataka, I have graduated from Govt. S.K.S.J.T.I, Bengaluru affiliated to V.T.U, Belagavi and completed my initial studies from Govt. High School and S.M Boomareddy Composite PU College, Gadag, Karnataka, India. Apart from science, I have artistic inclinations towards Music and Poetry.
